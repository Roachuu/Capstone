About ACLC Library

The Library provides a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and other resources related to information technology and computer science. It serves as a learning resource for students enrolled in the AMA Computer Learning Center and as a resource for the wider community of professionals and researchers in the field of information technology. The library may offer a range of services and facilities, such as access to online databases, a quiet study area, and computers with internet access.
